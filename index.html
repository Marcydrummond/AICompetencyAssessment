Educator AI Competency Self-Assessment

This assessment will help determine your current AI competency level based on the Educator AI Competency Framework and at the conclusion will provide you with a personal profile.

1. Technical AI Skills

These questions assess your ability to use and understand generative AI and other AI-enhanced educational tools

Q1.1: How would you rate your familiarity with using generative AI tools (like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot) in educational settings?

Q1.2: How do you interact with AI features embedded in educational technologies your institution uses?

Q1.3: How comfortable are you with evaluating and improving the quality of AI-generated content?

2. AI Implementation and Integration

These questions assess how you incorporate AI into your work

Q2.1: How frequently do you integrate AI tools into your work?

Q2.2: Which statement best describes how you implement AI?

Q2.3: How do you approach tasks, work, creation activities, etc. in relation to AI?

3. AI Literacy and Awareness

These questions assess understanding of AI concepts, capabilities, and limitations

Q3.1: How would you rate your understanding of AI concepts and terminology?

Q3.2: How well do you understand AI's limitations and potential biases?

Q3.3: How do you stay informed about developments in AI for education?

4. Responsible AI Use

These questions assess awareness and application of AI responsible use principles

Q4.1: How would you rate your knowledge of responsible AI use?

Q4.2: How do you address privacy and data security when using AI tools?

Q4.3: How do you address biases, digital equity, and digital accessibility when implementing AI tools?

5. AI Leadership and Collaboration

These questions assess how you influence others in AI adoption

Q5.1: How often do you collaborate with colleagues on AI implementation?

Q5.2: How do you contribute to others' AI knowledge and skills?

Q5.3: What role do you play in shaping AI policies or practices in your role/institution?

6. Innovation with AI

These questions assess how you, through the use of AI, innovate personal/institutional practices or processes to improve student and/or institutional outcomes

Q6.1: How do you use AI to adapt or innovate?

Q6.2: How do you adapt your practice based on insights from AI tools?

Q6.3: How do you measure the impact of AI implementation on outcomes?
